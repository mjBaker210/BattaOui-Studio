M. J. Baker

"From the time we're born until we die, we're kept busy with artificial stuff that isn't important."

I am not a professional photographer, but I like to take pictures. From taking photos of the great sphinx in Egypt to the Texas canyon in Arizona, always capturing the moment has been so special to me. In 2014 I was working in a transportation company out of Dallas, Texas as an over the road driver. It was very challenging job on daily basis. It gave me the sense of freedom, freedom of the open road. Nothing compares to be by yourself in the middle of nowhere and so quiet. It was a new birth to me. The pictures I took was by my iPhone 5 and most of the shoots I took was taking while I am driving an 18-wheeler. I used adobe photoshop and Lightroom to edit some of them. Thank you for reading.
